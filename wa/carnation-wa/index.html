Carnation, WA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Carnation is a city in King County, Washington, United States. The population was 2,158 at the 2020 census. It was historically known as Tolt (Lushootseed: tultxʷ) and lies at the confluence of the Snoqualmie and Tolt rivers. The city is located east of Redmond and south of Duvall on State Route 203.
