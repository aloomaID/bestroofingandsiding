One of the top choices for Miami’s climate is fiber cement siding. Known for its resilience, fiber cement siding resists moisture, heat, and pests, making it a top contender for homes in humid, storm-prone areas. It also offers excellent fire resistance, which is crucial during hurricane season.

Pleasure Point, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Pleasure Point is a census-designated place (CDP) in Santa Cruz County, California. Pleasure Point sits at an elevation of 33 feet (10 m). The 2020 United States census reported Pleasure Point's population was 5,821. Prior to the 2010 census, this CDP was called Opal Cliffs.
