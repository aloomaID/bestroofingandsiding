Fiber cement is one of the most popular siding choices in Closter New Jersey due to its resilience against heat, moisture, and wind. Vinyl Siding
Vinyl siding remains a top contender in Closter New Jersey due to its affordability, low maintenance, and impressive weather resistance. Modern vinyl siding comes in a variety of styles and colors, making it easy to match your home’s aesthetic while protecting it from UV rays and moisture damage.
Wood Siding
For those who prefer a more natural look, wood siding can be a great option. However, in Closter New Jersey it’s crucial to opt for treated or cedar wood, which naturally resists moisture and insects. Regular maintenance and staining are required to prevent rotting.
