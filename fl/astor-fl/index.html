Vinyl Siding: Vinyl siding is a popular choice in Astor Florida due to its ability to resist moisture and mold. It's a cost-effective option that provides good insulation, ensuring that your home stays energy-efficient despite the heat.

Wood Siding: If you prefer a natural look, treated wood siding can work well in Astor Florida as long as it's properly sealed and maintained. Choose wood varieties resistant to moisture and pests for optimal performance in this tropical climate.

Astor, FL Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Astor is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) in Lake County, Florida, United States, located on the west side of the St. Johns River between Lake George and Lake Dexter. The community straddles State Road 40, with the community of Volusia in Volusia County across the St. Johns to the east and Astor Park to the west. The population was 1,556 at the 2010 census. It is part of the Orlando–Kissimmee–Sanford Metropolitan Statistical Area.
