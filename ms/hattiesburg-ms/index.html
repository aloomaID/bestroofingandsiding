Fiber Cement Siding
Fiber cement is one of the most popular siding choices in Hattiesburg Mississippi due to its resilience against heat, moisture, and wind. Vinyl Siding
Vinyl siding remains a top contender in Hattiesburg Mississippi due to its affordability, low maintenance, and impressive weather resistance. Wood Siding
For those who prefer a more natural look, wood siding can be a great option. However, in Hattiesburg Mississippi it’s crucial to opt for treated or cedar wood, which naturally resists moisture and insects. Regular maintenance and staining are required to prevent rotting.
Metal Siding
Metal siding, particularly aluminum and steel, is increasingly popular in Hattiesburg Mississippi for its durability and resistance to extreme weather conditions. It’s also highly energy-efficient, reflecting sunlight and keeping your home cooler during the hot summer months.

Hattiesburg, MS Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Hattiesburg is the fifth-largest city in the U.S. state of Mississippi, located primarily in Forrest County (where it is the county seat and largest city) and extending west into Lamar County. The city population was 45,989 at the 2010 census, with the population now being 48,730 in 2020, making it the state's fifth-most populous city. Hattiesburg is the principal city of the Hattiesburg Metropolitan Statistical Area, which encompasses Covington, Forrest, Lamar, and Perry counties. The city is located in the Pine Belt region.
